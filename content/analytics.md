*All market-size figures are estimates unless labeled "measured." Where sources conflict, ranges are shown. Treat TAM/SAM/SOM math as order-of-magnitude and assumption-driven.*

## The Core Problem (Measured)

AROYA's own cultivation guide tells growers to record runoff by hand:

> *"It is good practice to check and record the Volume, PH and EC of the leachate. This can be recorded in AROYA using Manual Readings."*

Even on the most advanced precision-agriculture cannabis platform, per-zone runoff chemistry remains a **manual bucket-and-meter task**. AROYA's automated Drip and Drain Station covers volume and EC only on a combined (non-isolated) drain stream; pH sensor launched Spring 2026; **no per-zone isolation** on any competitor device.

> **Interpretation:** The standalone revenue opportunity is modest. The investable thesis is **strategic value** — Root View as a defensive/feature acquisition for an established platform like AROYA, not as a large standalone TAM play.

## Royalty Structure Benchmarks *(Measured ranges from published licenses)*

- Patent royalties broadly: **0.1–25%** (cross-industry median ~5%)
- Electronics/hardware: **1–5%** of end-product selling price
- Common illustrative structure: **$100K upfront + 5% of net sales**
- SEC filed example: **25% of gross revenues + $50K annual minimum**

**Preferred exit structure:** partial buy + royalty licensing. Fall-back: exclusive license with upfront + 5–8% royalty + annual minimum.

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | What It Does | Gap vs. Root View |
|---|---|---|
| **AROYA Drip and Drain** (Addium/METER) | Automated EC + volume + temperature on combined drain stream | No per-zone isolation; pH launching Spring 2026; no volume isolation |
| **Runoff Copilot** (Soil Moisture Sense, UK) | Volume monitoring for entire bag runs; optional EC/pH; solar, cellular | Aggregate run volume only; not per-zone sequential isolation; horticulture focus |
| **TrolMaster Aqua-X** | Substrate WC + irrigation control (up to ~30 zones) | No automated runoff-chemistry isolation |
| **Growlink** | TDR substrate WC/EC + IoT controllers | No runoff chemistry isolation |
| **Bluelab Combo Meter** ($339) | Industry-standard manual pH + EC meter | Manual only — exactly the workflow Root View automates |
| **Pulse / Trym / Argus / Priva** | Environmental/substrate monitoring | None perform automated per-zone runoff EC/pH sampling |

**Root View's differentiation:** sequential per-zone isolation — one zone's runoff sampled at a time, samples never mix — yielding clean EC + pH + volume per zone. No competitor does this today.
